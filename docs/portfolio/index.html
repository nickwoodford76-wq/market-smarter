04 Anzu · Editorial strategy

Creating a thought leader

Anzu wanted to grow its brand, share of voice and authority while educating audiences on in-game advertising. I launched and ran the Anzu blog with a clear strategy and schedule, keeping articles engaging, on-brand and never too salesy.

05 Unruly · Data storytelling

Telling stories through data

One of Unruly’s products could tell advertisers which emotions viewers felt while watching their ads, through a mix of technology and surveying. I mined the internal database for unique stories, many of which earned big attention across the trade and national press.

I turned the same insight into infographics, videos, blog posts and interactive landing pages, including ranked round-ups of the most emotional ads for moments like Christmas and the Super Bowl.
